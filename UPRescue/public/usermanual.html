Step 0 - Count 5 Mississippi so that radio buttons become enabled; it takes time to get your location :)

Step 1 - Choose what resource you would like and then press submit

Step 2 - The locations are marked 1-5; 1 being the closest and 5 being the farthest

Step 3 - Click the location you like and then the address should appear in the top search bar

Step 4 - Click the search bar and press the address in the dropdown and the Map should take you there

Step 5 - If you click on the place in the map you can go directly to google maps for directions
